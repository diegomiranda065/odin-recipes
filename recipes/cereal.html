Cereal With Milk

This is a recipe to make a simple bowl of cereal. I like this recipe because it is one of those things you can whip up in a matter of minutes. Cereal with milk is one of those slept on dishes that only true alpha males eat because it saves time and money so you can focus on your true passion which is making money.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of choice
  • Milk

*optional* lactose free milk if your tummy hurts when you drink normal cows milk. (nothing wrong with that)

Steps

  1. Grab your favorite bowl
  2. Pour cereal into the bowl
  3. Pour milk
  4. Grab spoon
  5. Find youtube video that you can eat to and enjoy your delicious bowl of cereal
