Queso Fresco Taco

Home

This is a recipe to make a taco with queso fresco. This is a personal favorite of mine because it is two of my favorite things: tortillas and queso fresco. This one is good when you are broke and bored and want to eat something quick and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Queso Fresco
  • Tortilla of your choice

Steps

  1. Get tortilla
  2. Throw it on stove top and cook both sides until warm and soft
  3. Cut a slice of cheese
  4. Roll up your cheese in the tortilla
  5. Find youtube video that you can eat to and enjoy your taco
