Pepperoni Pizza

Home

This is a recipe to make a simple pepperoni pizza. After following this recipe you will know how to make a pepperoni pizza and share it with all of your best friends.

Ingredients

  • Ball of dough
  • Red sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Lay flour onto pizza board
  2. Knead dough into cute pizza shape
  3. Spread red sauce over dough
  4. Sprinkle desired amount of cheese over red sauce
  5. Add pepperonis
  6. Toss that thang into the oven
  7. Pizza is ready
